FoodBuddies Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

FoodBuddies ("we", "our") is a group ordering app. This policy describes how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

When you use the app, we may collect:

  • Order information: name used for pickup, contact (email/phone/WeChat), meal choices, dietary preferences, and other details you enter for your order.
  • Payment proof: if you upload a payment screenshot, it is stored to verify payment for the group order.
  • Device information: we may use a device token to send you push notifications about your order (e.g. when the group order is confirmed).

2. How We Use It

We use this information to:

  • Fulfill and manage group orders with the restaurant.
  • Contact you about pickup time and location.
  • Send push notifications related to your order.

3. Storage & Security

Order data and payment images are stored using Firebase (Google). We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Your Choices

You can choose what to provide in the app. If you do not want push notifications, you can disable them in your device settings.

5. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will change when we do.
